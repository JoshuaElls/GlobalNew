Our Foundation

Our Story

Global Interconnect, Inc. (Gii) was founded in 1995 in Pocasset, Massachusetts, bringing together industry veterans with a shared vision: to build a precision manufacturing partner that could meet the uncompromising demands of the medical device industry. What began as a specialized connector manufacturer has evolved into a comprehensive contract manufacturing enterprise serving the world's leading OEMs.

Today, Gii leverages over 40 years of combined leadership experience to deliver cutting-edge solutions across custom connectors, cable assemblies, and electromechanical devices. Our US-Asia based engineering, supply chain, and manufacturing infrastructure spans 1.5 million square feet of production capacity, including 15,000 square feet of ISO 8 clean room facilities — all built on a foundation of FDA registration and ISO 13485 + ISO 9001 certification.
