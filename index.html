Who We Are

Your Partner in Medical Device Contract Manufacturing

Global Interconnect, Inc. (Gii) is a US-Asia based engineering, supply chain, and manufacturing firm with a primary focus on custom connector, cable, and electromechanical assemblies for medical devices and high-end industrial OEMs.

We specialize in energy-based and diagnostic medical devices — including scopes, probes, and catheters — where zero-failure performance is the only acceptable standard. As an FDA-registered, ISO 13485 and ISO 9001 certified manufacturer, we are uniquely qualified to deliver consistent quality at scale.

What We Build

Core Manufacturing Capabilities

End-to-end manufacturing expertise covering every stage of your medical device's production lifecycle.

Electromechanical Assembly

Full device and sub-assembly manufacturing with rigorous in-process quality controls, supporting complex medical device builds from prototype to volume production.

Custom Connector Molding

Precision injection molding of custom medical connector housings and overmolds — engineered for biocompatibility, durability, and exact-fit performance.

Coating & Surface Treatment

Specialized coating processes for lubricity, biocompatibility, and electrical performance — meeting the demanding requirements of single-use and reusable devices.

PCB Assembly (PCBA)

Surface-mount and through-hole PCB assembly for medical-grade electronics, with full traceability and IPC Class 2/3 quality standards.

Metal Stamping & Fabrication

High-precision metal stamping, forming, and fabrication for connector contacts, shields, and structural components — tight tolerances at volume scale.
