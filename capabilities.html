Custom Connector Design & Molding

Precision injection molding of custom medical connector housings and overmolds engineered for biocompatibility, durability, and exact-fit performance. Whether you need push-pull, locking, non-locking, or breakaway configurations, our design and molding expertise ensures your connectors meet the most demanding performance and regulatory requirements.

  • Biocompatible material selection and validation
  • Push-pull, locking, and non-locking configurations
  • Backward compatible design engineering
  • Miniaturization and tight tolerance capabilities
  • Full DFM review and process validation

Cable Assembly Manufacturing

Complete cable assembly manufacturing from wire cutting through final test. Gii handles every step of the process with precision and traceability, offering custom extruded wires, cables, and tubing in high-flex, shielded, coaxial, and multi-conductor options. Every assembly receives 100% electrical testing to ensure zero-failure performance.

  • Custom wire extrusion and formulation
  • High-flex, shielded, and coaxial options
  • Multi-conductor cable capabilities
  • 100% electrical test coverage
  • Full traceability and documentation

Electromechanical Assembly

Full device and sub-assembly manufacturing with rigorous in-process quality controls at every stage. Our electromechanical assembly capabilities handle complex multi-component assemblies, supporting everything from simple single-component builds to intricate device-level production.

  • Complex multi-component assemblies
  • Full device-level builds
  • In-process quality controls at every stage
  • Prototype through volume production
  • Zero-defect manufacturing principles

PCB Assembly (PCBA)

Surface-mount and through-hole PCB assembly for medical-grade electronics. Our PCBA capabilities meet IPC Class 2/3 standards with full component traceability. Advanced automated optical inspection (AOI) and X-ray inspection ensure the highest quality standards for your electronics manufacturing.

  • SMT and through-hole assembly
  • IPC Class 2/3 standards compliance
  • Full component traceability
  • AOI and X-ray inspection
  • Medical-grade quality assurance

Metal Stamping & Machining

Precision stamping, forming, and CNC machining for connector contacts, shields, housings, and structural components. Gii maintains tight tolerances at volume scale, delivering metal components that meet the exacting requirements of medical device applications.

  • Precision stamping and forming
  • CNC machining capabilities
  • Connector contacts and shields
  • Tight tolerances at volume scale
  • Material expertise and supply chain

Coating & Surface Treatment

Specialized coating and surface treatment processes engineered for the unique requirements of medical devices. From lubricity coatings to biocompatible treatments and electrical performance coatings, Gii supports both single-use and reusable device applications with precision and consistency.

  • Lubricity coatings for device insertion
  • Biocompatible surface treatments
  • Electrical performance coatings
  • Single-use and reusable device support
  • Process validation and documentation

Clean Room Manufacturing

State-of-the-art clean room facilities totaling 15,000 square feet of ISO 8 rated space.   • Sterile device manufacturing support
  • Critical device production capabilities
  • Environmental monitoring and controls

Transferring Manufacturing to Gii

Gii specializes in seamless manufacturing transfers from other contract manufacturers or from OEM internal production environments. Our experienced engineering and operations team handles every aspect of the transition to minimize disruption and ensure continuity of supply.

Our transfer process includes:

  • Comprehensive DFM review and optimization
  • Process validation and qualification
  • Supply chain transition and vendor qualification
  • Equipment setup and process documentation
  • Trial runs and ramp-up support
  • Full traceability and regulatory documentation

Whether you're consolidating suppliers, seeking cost optimization, or preparing for growth, Gii's transfer expertise ensures your device maintains the same quality, performance, and regulatory compliance you've established with your current manufacturer.
